Graduated with a filmmaking degree in 2010, Severin has been directing short films and commercials ever since. Recently, he worked on a Dior & Mojeh clip produced by La Onda. Polymorphic director, he knows how to tell each story accurately, in a constantly renewed aesthetic concern.


DOP trained at Louis Lumière institute, Elie has worked on twenty short films in the last five years. Recently he has been noticed for his work on music videos produced by Blogothèque for Sigur Rós, The Dø, Gonzales and Alt J and collaborated on Céline Sciamma’s project Tomboy. He also directed a clip for The Staves. Talented, inspired, Elie deals with the circumstances to find the single defining image in each project.


Elie Girard : "The Do : a take away show", produced by La Blogothèque / "The Staves : Teeth White", uncredited producer


DOP and director, Andy made his debut as a director freestyle ski movies (2 nominations in 2012 and 2013 at the IF3 Freeski Film Festival) before turning to music videos and advertising. Recently, he has worked with the directing duo Julien + Adrien for Rone and Nuits Sonores festival clips. As he goes along, Andy demonstrates impressive sensitivity and aesthetic through his projects.


Andy Collet : "Les Temps changent", produced by Collet Brothers Channel / "Rone : Quitter la ville", DOP: Andy Collet, directed by Julien+Adrien, produced by Partizan
